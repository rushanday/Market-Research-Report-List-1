<p><p>The Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide (FTO) market comprises two primary types: N-Type and P-Type. N-Type FTO, rich in electrons, is commonly used in applications requiring high conductivity, such as transparent conductive coatings in photovoltaics and displays. P-Type FTO, which has holes as the majority charge carriers, is less prevalent and mainly utilized in specialized applications like certain types of OLEDs. Both types are essential in advanced electronic devices, offering distinct electrical properties to enhance performance.</p></p>
