<p><ul><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time of Flight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D sensor array market primarily includes two types: Structured Light and Time of Flight (ToF). Structured Light uses projected patterns to capture 3D information, making it ideal for applications requiring high precision, such as facial recognition and industrial automation. In contrast, Time of Flight measures the time taken by light to bounce back from surfaces, enabling rapid distance calculations suitable for mobile devices and robotics. Both technologies are vital for enhancing depth perception in various industries.</p></p>
