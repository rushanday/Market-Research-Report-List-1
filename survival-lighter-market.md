<p><strong>The Survival Lighter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Lighter</li><li>Fuel Lighter</li><li>Plasma Lighter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The survival lighter market is categorized into three main types: electric lighters, fuel lighters, and plasma lighters. Electric lighters use rechargeable batteries and create a flame through an electric arc, making them windproof and suitable for various conditions. Fuel lighters operate using flammable gas or liquid, providing a traditional flame source, often favored for reliability. Plasma lighters generate a high-voltage arc that ignites materials without a flame, offering a modern, environmentally-friendly alternative ideal for outdoor survival scenarios.</p></p>
