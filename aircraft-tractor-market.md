<p><ul><li>Conventional Tractors</li><li>Towbarless Tractors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft tractor market is primarily divided into two types: conventional tractors and towbarless tractors. Conventional tractors utilize a towbar to connect with the aircraft for towing, offering ease of use and reliability in various airport settings. In contrast, towbarless tractors operate without a towbar, providing enhanced maneuverability and reducing the risk of damage to aircraft during pushback and towing. Both types serve essential roles in airport operations, facilitating aircraft movement and contributing to operational efficiency.</p></p>
