<p><ul><li>Excimer Laser</li><li>Femtosecond Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser vision correction market primarily includes two main types: Excimer lasers and Femtosecond lasers. Excimer lasers utilize ultraviolet light to reshape the cornea, effectively treating refractive errors like myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Femtosecond lasers, on the other hand, employ short laser pulses to create precise corneal flaps for procedures such as LASIK. Both technologies enhance visual acuity and have contributed significantly to the growing demand for corrective eye surgery in the global healthcare market.</p></p>
