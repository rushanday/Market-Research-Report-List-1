<p><strong>The Ship Radar Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>S-band</li><li>X-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ship radar scanners operate primarily in two frequency bands: S-band and X-band. S-band radars, typically operating between 2 to 4 GHz, offer longer-range detection and are less affected by rain, making them ideal for long-distance maritime navigation and surveillance. Conversely, X-band radars, operating between 8 to 12 GHz, provide higher resolution imaging and are better for detecting smaller objects or targets, such as other vessels or debris, at shorter ranges, making them suitable for coastal and port operations.</p></p>
