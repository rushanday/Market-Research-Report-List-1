<p><p>The Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) analyzers market features two primary types: Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers and Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers. Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers utilize filters to isolate specific wavelengths of light for detecting enzyme reactions, ensuring reliability in quantifying analyte concentrations. In contrast, Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers employ diffraction gratings to disperse light, allowing for precise measurement across a broader wavelength range, enhancing sensitivity and accuracy in immunoassay results. Both types are essential in clinical diagnostics and research applications.</p></p>
