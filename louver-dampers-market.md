<p><p>The louver dampers market is categorized into three main types: manual, electric, and pneumatic. Manual louver dampers are operated by hand, allowing for direct, user-controlled airflow regulation. Electric louver dampers utilize electric actuators for automated control, providing convenience and precision in airflow management. Pneumatic louver dampers use compressed air to operate, enabling efficient and rapid adjustments in ventilation systems. These types cater to diverse applications in industrial, commercial, and residential settings, enhancing energy efficiency and air quality control.</p></p>
