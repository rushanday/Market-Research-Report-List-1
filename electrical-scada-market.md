<p><p>The electrical SCADA market encompasses several key components. Remote Terminal Units (RTUs) collect data from field devices and transmit it to the Master Terminal Unit (MTU), which manages the overall system. Human Machine Interfaces (HMIs) provide visual interaction for operators. Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) automate processes and control equipment. Communication Systems facilitate data exchange between components. Additionally, other devices like Intelligent Electronic Devices (IEDs), circuit relays, and sensors enhance system efficiency and monitoring capabilities within electrical networks.</p></p>
