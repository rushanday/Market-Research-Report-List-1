<p><strong>The EUV Light Sources Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Energetic Plasma</li><li>Synchrotron Radiation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EUV light sources market primarily includes two types: energetic plasma and synchrotron radiation. Energetic plasma sources utilize high-energy plasmas to produce extreme ultraviolet light, essential for advanced semiconductor lithography. These systems are compact and efficient, catering to the growing demand for microelectronic devices. In contrast, synchrotron radiation sources generate EUV light by accelerating electrons through magnetic fields, resulting in high-intensity and tunable light. They are used in various research applications but are larger and more complex than plasma sources.</p></p>
