<p><p>The Portable Dental Ultrasonic Scaler market features three main types: Sonic Scalers, Magnetostrictive Scalers, and Piezoelectric Scalers. Sonic Scalers utilize sound waves to produce vibrations for plaque removal, making them effective but less powerful. Magnetostrictive Scalers work by using magnetic fields to create vibrations in tips, providing thorough cleaning with a broad range of frequencies. Piezoelectric Scalers rely on ceramic crystals to generate ultrasonic vibrations, offering precision and efficiency, particularly in delicate procedures. Each type caters to specific dental needs and preferences.</p></p>
