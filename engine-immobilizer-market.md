<p><strong>The Engine Immobilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine immobilizer market comprises two main types: mechanical and electronic systems. Mechanical immobilizers utilize physical devices, such as steering locks or gear locks, to prevent vehicle operation without a specific key or mechanism, thereby offering a straightforward yet effective layer of security. In contrast, electronic immobilizers rely on advanced technology, often integrated with the vehicle's ignition system, utilizing transponders or key fobs to disrupt the engine start process if unauthorized access is attempted, providing a more sophisticated security solution.</p></p>
