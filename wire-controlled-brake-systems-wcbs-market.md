<p><p>Wire-Controlled Brake Systems (WCBS) utilize electronic signals instead of traditional mechanical linkages to control braking, enhancing responsiveness and safety. The Electro-Hydraulic Braking System (EHB) combines hydraulic and electronic technologies for rapid braking force application. The Electronic Mechanical Brake System (EMB) operates through electric actuation of the brake components, ensuring precise control and reduced weight. Other systems in the WCBS market may include hybrid technologies or advanced vehicle integration solutions, contributing to improved vehicle performance and efficiency.</p></p>
