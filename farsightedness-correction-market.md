<p><p>The farsightedness correction market includes innovative solutions like corneal inlays and scleral implants. Corneal inlays are small devices placed in the cornea to improve near vision, reshaping the eye’s focusing ability. In contrast, scleral implants involve positioning a prosthetic lens on the eye's surface to enhance vision, often used for patients with more severe conditions. Both technologies aim to reduce dependency on glasses or contact lenses, catering to a growing demand for effective vision correction solutions.</p></p>
