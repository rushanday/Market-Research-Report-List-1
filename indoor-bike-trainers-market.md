<p><p>Indoor bike trainers come in two main types: smart bike trainers and classic bike trainers. Smart bike trainers are advanced devices that connect to apps and devices via Bluetooth or ANT+, offering interactive features like real-time data tracking, virtual cycling experiences, and adjustable resistance based on terrain simulations. In contrast, classic bike trainers are simpler, often manual setups that provide a more basic riding experience without digital connectivity. Both options cater to cyclists seeking indoor training solutions but vary significantly in technology and functionality.</p></p>
