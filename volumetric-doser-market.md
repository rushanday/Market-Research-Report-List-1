<p><p>The volumetric doser market is divided into two main types: volumetric dosers and micro volumetric dosers. Volumetric dosers are designed for larger quantities of liquids or powders, utilizing a predefined volume measurement for dispensing, ideal for applications in food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. In contrast, micro volumetric dosers focus on precise, small-volume dispensing, catering to applications requiring high accuracy, such as in laboratories or specialized manufacturing. Both types enhance efficiency and consistency in production processes.</p></p>
