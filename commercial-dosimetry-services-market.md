<p><strong>The Commercial Dosimetry Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OSL Technology</li><li>TLD Technology</li><li>CR-39 Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Dosimetry Services Market encompasses various technologies for measuring radiation exposure. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) technology uses stored energy in materials, releasing light when stimulated, providing precise dose measurements. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeter) technology involves materials that emit light upon heating, indicating radiation exposure levels. CR-39 technology utilizes a polymer that registers radiation damage, enabling detection of alpha particles. Each technology serves distinct sectors, including healthcare, nuclear energy, and environmental monitoring, catering to diverse radiation measurement needs.</p></p>
