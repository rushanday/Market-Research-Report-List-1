<p><p>The stone water repellent treatments market is divided into two primary types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based treatments utilize a mixture of water and active chemical agents, offering an eco-friendly and less hazardous option. They penetrate stone surfaces effectively, providing protection against moisture while minimizing environmental impact. In contrast, solvent-based treatments employ organic solvents to enhance penetration and durability, offering longer-lasting protection under harsher conditions. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and application environments in stone preservation.</p></p>
