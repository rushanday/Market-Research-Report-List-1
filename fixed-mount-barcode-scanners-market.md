Cabled barcode readers are directly connected to a computer system, offering reliable performance and constant power supply. In contrast, wireless barcode readers utilize Bluetooth or Wi-Fi technology to transmit data, providing flexibility and mobility, allowing users to scan items from a distance without being tethered to a workstation. Both types enhance operational efficiency.</p></p>
