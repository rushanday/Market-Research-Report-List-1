<p><p>Metallised polypropylene (MPP) films are packaging materials that combine polypropylene with a metallic layer, enhancing barrier properties, aesthetics, and shelf life. The market is primarily divided into two types: metallised blow polypropylene films and metallised cast polypropylene films. Metallised blow polypropylene films are produced through a blowing process, offering superior strength and flexibility. In contrast, metallised cast polypropylene films are formed through casting, providing a smoother surface and improved clarity, making them suitable for various applications in packaging, labeling, and more.</p></p>
