Compound essential oils are blends of multiple essential oils formulated to achieve specific therapeutic effects or fragrances. These blends often target particular health concerns, enhancing their efficacy. On the other hand, unilateral essential oils consist of a single botanical source, offering the unique properties of that specific plant. Both types serve distinct purposes in aromatherapy and wellness practices, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
