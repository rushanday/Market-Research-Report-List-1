<p><p>Mass Flow Controllers (MFCs) are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for precise gas flow management. Thermal MFCs utilize a thermal sensor to measure gas flow based on cooling effects when gas passes over a heated element, providing accurate flow rates for critical processes. Pressure MFCs, on the other hand, maintain gas flow by regulating pressure differentials, ensuring stable delivery even with varying upstream conditions. Both types are crucial for maintaining the quality and consistency of semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
