<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are essential components in electronic devices, functioning as the backbone for connecting electronic components. The market is categorized into Single-sided, Double-sided, and Multi-layer PCBs. Single-sided PCBs have components on one side, ideal for simple circuits. Double-sided PCBs allow for more complex connections with components on both sides. Multi-layer PCBs contain multiple layers, enabling advanced configurations and higher component density, making them suitable for sophisticated electronics like smartphones and computers. Each type addresses different functional and design needs in the electronics market.</p></p>
