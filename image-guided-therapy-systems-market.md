<p><p>The Image-guided Therapy Systems market encompasses various imaging modalities that enhance precision in medical procedures. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides high-resolution images of soft tissues. Endoscopes allow for direct visualization within the body. X-ray Fluoroscopy offers continuous imaging for dynamic studies. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) focus on functional imaging, critical for oncology and cardiology.</p></p>
