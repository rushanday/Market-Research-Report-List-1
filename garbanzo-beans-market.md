<p><p>The garbanzo beans market is primarily divided into three types: Kabuli chickpeas, Desi chickpeas, and others. Kabuli chickpeas are larger, lighter in color, and often preferred for their smooth texture, making them popular in salads and hummus. Desi chickpeas are smaller, darker, and have a rougher skin, commonly used in stews and curries due to their robust flavor.
