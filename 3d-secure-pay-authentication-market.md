<p><p>The 3D Secure Pay Authentication market consists of two main types: Access Control Server (ACS) and Merchant Plug-in (MPI). ACS is a system used by banks to validate the identity of cardholders during online transactions, enhancing security by requesting additional verification. Conversely, the Merchant Plug-in acts as an interface between the merchant’s website and the ACS, facilitating secure payment processing. Together, they ensure a safer online shopping experience, reducing fraud and improving consumer trust in e-commerce.</p></p>
