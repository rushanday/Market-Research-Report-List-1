<p><p>The vacuum evaporators market comprises various types, primarily including rotary vacuum evaporators and centrifugal vacuum evaporators. Rotary vacuum evaporators utilize a rotating flask to enhance evaporation efficiency, providing uniform heating and reducing the boiling point of solvents. In contrast, centrifugal vacuum evaporators use centrifugal force to accelerate the evaporation process, making them suitable for large-scale production. Both types are widely used in chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries for concentrating solutions, recovering solvents, and enhancing purification processes.</p></p>
