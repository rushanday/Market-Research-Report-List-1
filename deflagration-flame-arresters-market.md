<p><strong>The Deflagration Flame Arresters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>End-of-line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deflagration flame arresters are devices designed to prevent the propagation of flames in gas pipelines. The market for these arresters is segmented into two main types: in-line and end-of-line. </p><p>In-line arresters are installed within a pipeline, allowing gases to flow continuously while mitigating flame risks throughout the system. Conversely, end-of-line arresters are placed at the termination points of pipelines, providing protection against backflash and ensuring safety at the exit of the system. Both types are crucial for managing explosion hazards in various industrial applications.</p></p>
