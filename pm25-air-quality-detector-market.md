<p><p>The PM2.5 air quality detector market comprises various technologies, including TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitors, which provide continuous measurements by collecting particles on a filter. Beta attenuation monitors utilize radioactive isotopes to assess particulate concentration by measuring the attenuation of beta particles as they pass through collected samples. Other types include optical monitors that use laser technology for real-time analysis. Each technology offers distinct advantages for monitoring fine particulate matter, critical for assessing air quality and public health.</p></p>
