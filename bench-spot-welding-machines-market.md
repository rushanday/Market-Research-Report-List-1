<p><p>Bench spot welding machines are categorized into three main types based on their operation. Manual welding machines require an operator to initiate and control the welding process, offering flexibility for small projects. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process, reducing operator input while still requiring some manual adjustments, making them suitable for medium-scale production. Automatic welding machines fully automate the spot welding process, maximizing efficiency and consistency, ideal for high-volume manufacturing environments where precision and speed are critical.</p></p>
