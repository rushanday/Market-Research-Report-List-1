<p><p>The Disposable Hemodynamic Monitoring Product Market is categorized into three types: invasive, minimally invasive, and non-invasive monitoring. Invasive methods involve direct measurement of blood pressure and cardiac output via catheters inserted into the bloodstream. Minimally invasive techniques utilize percutaneous sensors or monitors to gather data with reduced patient discomfort. Non-invasive monitoring employs external devices like sensors or cuffs to assess hemodynamic parameters without penetrating the skin, offering a safer and more convenient option for continuous patient monitoring.</p></p>
